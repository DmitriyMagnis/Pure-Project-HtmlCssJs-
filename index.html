Steps for Creating Candles

Creating a soy wax candle can include the following steps:

  1. Preparation of materials: small pieces of soy wax, candle wick, fragrances, dyes, and other additional materials.

  2. Preparing the mold: selecting a mold for the candle and preparing it for filling with wax.

  3. Preparing the wax: melting the soy wax in a melting device to the desired temperature. Adding fragrances and dyes if needed.

  4. Pouring wax into the mold: pouring melted wax into the mold, holding the wick in the center of the candle.

  5. Cooling and hardening: cooling the candle until it completely hardens, then carefully removing it from the mold.
